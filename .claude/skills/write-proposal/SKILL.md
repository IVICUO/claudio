# Write proposal

IVICUO proposals follow a tight five section structure. Length: typically 3 to 5 pages, never more than 8.

## Structure

1. **Context**: one paragraph restating the client's situation as IVICUO understands it. This proves we listened.
2. **What we propose**: the work in plain prose. Lead with the outcome, not the activity. Two to four paragraphs.
3. **How we work**: the engagement model (team composition from CRM and Data branches as relevant, cadence, communication channels). Reference biweekly status calls and Slack as the working channel.
4. **Deliverables and timeline**: dated milestones in a short table. Use absolute dates, not "week 1, week 2".
5. **Investment**: pricing, payment cadence, what is and is not included.

## Tone rules

Professional, understated, no marketing voice.
No dashes anywhere in the document. Use commas or restructure.
First person plural ("we") for IVICUO, second person ("you") for the client.
Avoid "leverage", "unlock", "best in class", "synergy", "robust", "seamless".
Match the client's working language (most often Spanish or English, occasionally both in the same engagement).

## Before submitting

Read the document end to end once for tone, then once for accuracy of names, dates, numbers.
